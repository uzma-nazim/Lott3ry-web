FAQS




If 10,000 tickets were to be created, it would cause the price of a ticket to be too high. Lottery tickets are meant to not cost much. Little price for a huge payout! This makes it easier to pay for a ticket.

Each lottery ticket will cost 15 Matic, we want to keep the price per ticket around $20 USD. The amount of Matic for each lottery will differ depending on the price of Matic at the time of mint.

There will always be over 100 ETH in winnings! Each prize number is different and depending on the prize you win, will determine what you win!

The Web3 Lott3ry will take place on the polygon network to avoid paying high gas fees for the price to mint.

If you are the winner of one of the 25 prizes, to claim your prize you will simply transfer the ticket back to the Web3 Lott3ry wallet that you received it from. Once transferred Web3 Lott3ry will transfer you your prize.
For the free ticket winners. Simply leave the ticket in your wallet and you will automatically be given a free mint into the next lottery.

Prize winners are generated randomly. Each ticket is randomly generated into the 35,000 available. There is no telling who will get what. And yes, prizes CAN range all the way from 1 to 35,000.

Once you mint your NFT(s) you will see them on Opensea. The tickets will all be the same pre-reveal until all 35,000 have been sold. Once all 35,000 have been sold, you will be able to see if your ticket(s) is a winner!

Each lottery will all take place on the Web3 Lott3ry platform, but each lottery is different. Once one lottery is complete, the next lottery will begin minting.

No, all tickets will be made public at the same time. There will be no early access to the lottery. It is a first come first serve basis.
